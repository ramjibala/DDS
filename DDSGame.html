What is DDS?

Direct Data Solutions (DDS) is a comprehensive global industry data solution managed by ARC and IATA to provide carriers worldwide with access to high-quality market data. DDS includes detailed, segment-level itinerary and ticketing information sold through agencies and carrier channels.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive Data: ASP, BSP, and Carrier-contributed transactions
  • Cabin Classes: First Class, Business, Premium Economy, Discount Economy, and Non-Revenue
  • Product Versions: Marketplace (global) and Touches (filtered)
  • Delivery Options: Daily or Monthly via sFTP or iiNet
  • Data Quality: Fixed-row format, 620 characters per transaction

Data Processing Bases:

  • Processing Date: Transactions processed daily or monthly
  • Date of Departure: Settled transactions by calendar month

How to Play:

  1. Enter your name (your last name is remembered)
  2. Select the number of questions (5-50)
  3. Choose difficulty level: Easy, Medium, Hard, or Mixed
  4. Click an answer option to select it
    • Your answer is submitted immediately
    • The quiz automatically proceeds to the next question
  5. Use the Back button to review previous questions
  6. Complete all questions and receive your knowledge rating
  7. View your performance on the leaderboard

Rating System:

  • Expert - 90-100% correct (Mastered DDS!)
  • Advanced - 75-89% correct (Strong knowledge)
  • Intermediate - 60-74% correct (Good foundation)
  • Beginner - Below 60% (Keep learning!)
